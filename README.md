## Overview of Undergraduate thesis on auditory cortex
<i>
General idea: Cortical columns form a convolutional structure which encodes sounds in 3 steps <br>
'1' Core: pitch, <br>
'2' Belt: inflection (change over short timescales), <br>
'3' Parabelt: codon units – like phonemes which are then recombined in higher areas <br><br>
x) the codons link into Wernicke’s area, where they are recombined to form words from proto-syllables
</i>
